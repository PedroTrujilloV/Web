Here's a little bit about the couple :)

Leslie

About Her ▼▲



Leslie was born on Christmas Eve in 1984 in Winter Park, Florida. She graduated from high school in Orlando, Florida and moved to Cambridge, Massachusetts to study Aerospace Engineering at MIT. She also spent one awesome year abroad at the other Cambridge (the one in the UK)! Back in Florida, Leslie has worked as a systems engineer, high school math teacher, and now as a software engineer. Her two biggest enjoyments in life are traveling and reading, but she also enjoys "running" (not very fast), snow skiing, exploring Orlando with friends. She's excited to go on lots of new adventures with Pedro throughout the world :).

Pedro

About Him ▼▲



Pedro was born in the old Spanish colonial town of Honda, Tolima in Colombia. After finishing high school in Villavicencio, Pedro studied Systems Engineering at Unillanos, and now works in software development and 3d modeling and design. Although "work is play" for Pedro, he also enjoys soccer (futbol!), traveling, cars, and learning about technology.
